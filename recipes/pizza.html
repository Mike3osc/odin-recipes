Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the preferred dishes in the world and in this page you are going to learn how to prepare a pizza in 5 minutes

Pizza is rounded although you can find what it is called pizza al "tranzo" which is a pre-cut pizza with a squared design

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Chorizo de Leon
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. In the pizza base add some tomato sauce
  2. Add mozzarella cheese as much as you like
  3. Put on top generous slices of chorizo de leon
  4. Bake during 12 minutes in the oven
  5. Once cooked decorate with some rosemary leaves
