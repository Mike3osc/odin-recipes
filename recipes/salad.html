Summer Green Salad

Description

Now that summer heats as never, you can find here a recipe of a fresh green salad to get colder and refreshed

Summer is the most dangerous period of year where it is needed to drink a lot of liquids and it is important to eat softly

Ingredients

  • Lettuces
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Salt and vinegar
  • A glass of your favorite wine

Steps

  1. Clean under clear water the ingredients
  2. Chop them and put them in a tray
  3. Add salt and vinegar at you preference
  4. Mix everything with energy
  5. Take the glass of wine and drink it one shot, because you are the best
