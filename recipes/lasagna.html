Lasagna

Description

This is an italian lasagna cooked by brilliant chefs of the Milano School of Lasagna

This prestigious chefs use ten years of their life cooking for free to get a degree that will allow them to cook in a chinese wok restaurant

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Smashed meat
  • Tomato
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Life insurance

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Put past in the boiling water
  3. In a pan deep fry the meat and tomato with salt and pepper
  4. Put pasta leaves in a recipient
  5. Add meat to past and cover with new past layers
  6. Drink a beer because you have done it perfectly
